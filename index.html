System Information

How it works: Image Steganography is the practice of hiding secret data within an ordinary, non-secret file or message to avoid detection.

Example: Imagine you have a standard digital photo of a landscape. This tool encrypts your secret text using military-grade AES-256 encryption. It then embeds that encrypted data directly into the least significant bits (LSB) of the image's pixels.

To the naked eye, the new image looks exactly like the original. However, someone with the SecureStegano tool and your encryption password can extract and decrypt the hidden payload.

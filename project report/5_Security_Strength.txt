5. Security Strength & Cryptographic Accuracy (10 Marks)

The system utilizes AES-256, a globally recognized standard. Key generation is handled by PBKDF2 with sufficient iterations to deter offline dictionary attacks. The encryption module strictly segregates the Initialization Vector (IV) and Salt, maintaining absolute cryptographic accuracy. Exhaustive unit tests confirm an error-free encryption and decryption cycle, preventing data corruption during the transition state.
